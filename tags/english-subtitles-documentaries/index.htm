Documentaries with English subtitles

In an increasingly interconnected and globalised world, films are being shot in locations around the planet that were considered exotic just a couple decades ago. Viewers no longer have settle for what Hollywood produces, because documentaries with English subtitles are now available that explore cultures from all over the world. With quality translations RTD’s documentaries with English subtitles means language is no longer a barrier – you’ll be able to immediately understand speakers from any locale, from China and Russia to Brazil and Afghanistan… without the annoying dubbing.
